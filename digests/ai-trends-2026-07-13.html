OpenAI Ships GPT-Live, a Voice Model That Listens and Talks at the Same Time

GPT-Live-1, rolling out across ChatGPT's iOS, Android, and web apps, uses a full-duplex architecture that processes incoming and outgoing speech concurrently — it can backchannel with "mhmm," stay quiet while a user is thinking, or interrupt, deciding what to do many times per second. Complex requests get silently handed off to GPT-5.5 running in parallel; on OpenAI's own benchmark, its top reasoning setting jumps to 84.2% on GPQA versus 45.3% for the old Advanced Voice Mode.

Mistral's Robostral Navigate Steers Robots With Just One Camera
