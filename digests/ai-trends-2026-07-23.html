White House Directly Accuses Moonshot AI of Covertly Distilling Anthropic's Fable to Build Kimi K3

Michael Kratsios, director of the White House Office of Science and Technology Policy, said Wednesday the administration has evidence that Chinese lab Moonshot AI built an internal platform to systematically distill Anthropic's Fable model — rapidly rotating access methods to dodge detection — while developing its viral Kimi K3 system. It matters because it escalates a fight that was already simmering — Anthropic separately alleges Moonshot ran 3.4 million fraudulent Claude exchanges to extract reasoning and coding capability — into open, on-the-record US-China confrontation, days before Moonshot is set to release Kimi K3's full weights on July 27 as the largest open-weight model ever built. No sanctions have been announced yet and Moonshot hasn't responded, but Treasury has reportedly floated the threat.
